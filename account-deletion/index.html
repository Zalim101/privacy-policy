Delete Your HR Course Finder Account

We're sorry to see you go. If you wish to delete your HR Course Finder account and all associated data, please follow the instructions below.

How to Request Account Deletion

Please send an email to:

Email: info@hrconsultantslondon.com

In your email, please include:

  • Your registered email address
  • Subject line: "Account Deletion Request"
  • Any additional context (optional)

What Gets Deleted

When you request account deletion, we will permanently remove:

  • Your user profile and authentication credentials
  • Your saved favorite courses
  • Any personal preferences or settings
  • All data associated with your account

Processing Time

We will process your deletion request within 48 hours of receiving your email. You will receive a confirmation email once your account has been deleted.

Note

Account deletion is permanent and cannot be undone. If you wish to use HR Course Finder again in the future, you will need to create a new account.
